About
WHO I AM ?
Teuber-HS is a Home Security System that is used to control access for Entry to the Premises. It also serves as a Survailance System to Keep track of who comes and goes in a form of Video and Image Capture.
It shall operate both online and offline , as well as during Power Outages . It is an affordable Security System that is easy to install and easy to use . It will use a Camera Module , an RFID module and a Human-Machine Interface.
Owners shall have the Capability to monitor their homes as well as have Access on Electrical Locks during Power Outages. Teuber-HS will provide dependable Security and Survailance
